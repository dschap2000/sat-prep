SAT Math Reference

Area (A) & Circumference (C)

Circle Area: A = πr² Circle Circ: C = 2πr Rectangle: A = lw Triangle: A = ½bh

Right Triangles

Pythagorean: a² + b² = c² 30-60-90: x, x√3, 2x 45-45-90: s, s, s√2

Volume (V)

Rectangular Prism: V = lwh Cylinder: V = πr²h Sphere: V = (4/3)πr³ Cone: V = (1/3)πr²h Pyramid: V = (1/3)lwh

Circles & Angles

  • Degrees of arc in a circle: 360
  • Radians of arc in a circle: 2π
  • Sum of degrees in a triangle: 180
