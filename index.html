SAT Math Reference

Area (A) & Circumference (C)

Circle Area: \(A = \pi r^2\) Circle Circ: \(C = 2\pi r\) Rectangle: \(A = lw\) Triangle: \(A = \frac{1}{2}bh\)

Right Triangles

Pythagorean: \(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\) 30-60-90: \(x, x\sqrt{3}, 2x\) 45-45-90: \(s, s, s\sqrt{2}\)

Volume (V)

Rect. Prism: \(V = lwh\) Cylinder: \(V = \pi r^2h\) Sphere: \(V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3\) Cone: \(V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2h\) Pyramid: \(V = \frac{1}{3}lwh\)
